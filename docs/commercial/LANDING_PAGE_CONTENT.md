## Section 3 — The Solution (Platform Overview)

### EN

**Heading:** One platform. Every department. Full control.

**Subheading:** SmartPRO connects your entire operation — so your team spends less time on admin and more time running the business.

**Module cards (6):**

**HR Management**
Employees, leave, attendance, performance, and task workflows. Arabic HR letters generated in one click.

**Payroll**
WPS-compliant processing. Automatic PASI calculations for Omani nationals. Pay-slips in Arabic and English delivered to each employee's portal.

**Compliance**
Sanad expat quota tracking. MoL workforce reports. Omanization ratio dashboards. Know your compliance status before the inspector does.

**Documents**
Secure document vault with expiry reminders. Generate MoL-standard letters in seconds. Version control and approval workflows.

**Contracts**
Draft, send, e-sign, and archive. NDAs, service agreements, and quotations — with full audit trail.

**Finance**
P&L dashboards, cost-centre analysis, and executive KPI summaries. External auditor access built in.
